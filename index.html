Food & Culture

From Jollof rice wars to colorful festivals like Homowo and Aboakyer, Ghana’s culture is rich and flavorful—literally and spiritually.

  • 🍛 Jollof, Banku, and Fufu
  • 🎭 Festivals: Homowo, Chale Wote Street Art, Aboakyer
  • 🎶 Highlife & Azonto Vibes

Travel Tips

  • ✈️ Best time to visit: October to March (dry season)
  • 💬 Language: English + local dialects (Twi, Ga, Ewe)
  • 🚖 Transport: Uber, Trotro (minibuses), car rentals
  • 💸 Currency: Ghana Cedi (GHS)
  • 🧼 Safety: Ghana is generally safe—just stay alert in crowded areas.
